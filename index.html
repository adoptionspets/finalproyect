Adoption pets

The overpopulation of stray dogs and cats represents a critical challenge for urban sustainability, impacting public health, the environment, and social cohesion. Today in Mexico there are 29.7 millions of dogs and cats abandoned on the streets (Forbes,2025) Pets abandonment negatively affects the ecological balance increasing with wildlife and generating competition for resources. Some of the health problems that provoques this overpopulation on the streets are:

  • Rabies
  • Rickettsiosis
  • Leptospirosis
